About

Researcher in mathematics at the Tallinn Institute of Technology. My research interests tend to revolve around category theory — often enriched category theory — and its relation to other areas of mathematics such as optimal transport theory and linear algebra. Much of my research uses graphical languages, and I have recently started working more on synthetic probability and graphical representations of logics.
