About

Researcher in mathematics at the Tallinn Institute of Technology. My research interests revolve around category theory — typically enriched category theory — and its relation to other areas of mathematics such as optimal transport theory and linear algebra. Much of my work uses graphical languages, and I have recently been working on synthetic probability and graphical representations of logics.
